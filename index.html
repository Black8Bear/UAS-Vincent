Services

Our team of professionals will handle your items with utmost care. We provide full packing and unpacking services to ensure a stress-free move. From delicate china to bulky furniture, we’ve got you covered.

House Moving

Our house moving service is designed to handle all aspects of your residential relocation. From packing and loading to transportation and unpacking, we ensure a smooth and stress-free move for your family.

Apartment Moving

Moving in or out of an apartment can be tricky with tight spaces and often multiple flights of stairs. Our team is experienced in navigating these challenges to provide a seamless apartment moving experience.

Building Moving

Our building moving service is for those who literally want to move their entire building to a new location. We work with engineers and specialists to plan and execute the move safely and effectively. This service is subject to feasibility and safety assessments.

Company Moving

We offer comprehensive company moving services to ensure minimal disruption to your business operations. Our team is skilled in packing and moving office equipment, furniture, and sensitive documents securely and efficiently.

Frequently Asked Questions

A Comprehensive Guide to Avoiding Problems and Embracing Opportunities

What services do you offer?

We offer a comprehensive range of services including packing, loading, transporting, unloading, and unpacking. We also provide additional services such as furniture disassembly and assembly, and temporary storage

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, we are fully licensed and insured. We adhere to all federal and state regulations to ensure the safety and security of your belongings during the move

How do you determine the cost of a move?

The cost of a move is determined based on factors such as the distance of the move, the weight of the items being moved, and any additional services you choose.

How far in advance should I book your services?

We recommend booking our services at least 4-6 weeks in advance to ensure availability. However, we understand that sometimes moves can be unexpected and we will do our best to accommodate last-minute bookings.

What measures do you take to protect my belongings during the move?

We use high-quality packing materials and techniques to ensure the safety of your belongings. Our team is trained in handling all types of items, including fragile and valuable ones.

What if something gets damaged during the move?

While we take every precaution to prevent damage, in the unlikely event that something does get damaged, we have insurance coverage that will compensate you for the loss.
